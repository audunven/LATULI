Ontology Specification Draft
The M3 ontology describes entities related to freight transport and terminal operations.
The M3 ontology is developed in the research project Latuli, a project funded by the Norwegian Research Council. The partners in this project are Mixmove (https://www.mixmove.io/) and SINTEF (http://www.sintef.com). The main objective of this project is to perform data analysis and thereby get a step closer to self-learning freight terminals.

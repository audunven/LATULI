Ontology Specification Draft
The TCM (Terminal Capacity Measurement) ontology describes entities related to freight terminal characteristics and operations.
The TCM (Terminal Capacity Measurement) ontology is developed in the research project Latuli, a project funded by the Norwegian Research Council. The partners in this project are Mixmove (https://www.mixmove.io/) and SINTEF (http://www.sintef.com). The main objective of this project is to perform data analysis and thereby get a step closer to self-learning freight terminals.

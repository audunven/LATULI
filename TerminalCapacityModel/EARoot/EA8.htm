Class   		Name:  
  		Name: UnloadingTimePerPeriod
 
Details:
The time it takes to unload a transport means at the terminal. In order to accelerate the turnover of goods and free the doors as soon as possible, Boysen and Fliedner (REF) propose to minimize the sum of the completion times of the trucks. Minimizing the total time spent at the outbound docks by the outbound trucks is also a possible objective.<br/>
